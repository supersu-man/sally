Why Sally?
Calculating the exact amounts each person owes and the necessary transactions to settle shared expenses can be a complex and tedious task, often requiring spreadsheets. This complexity increases when some community expenses are not shared by everyone.
That's why we created Sally, offering an easy, free, fast, and reliable solution to divide group expenses after holidays, parties, shared gifts, festivals, vacations, trips, weddings, road trips, clubs, shared apartments, and more.
